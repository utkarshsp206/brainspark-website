Accessibility Auditing

SPZ has a team of experienced accessibility auditors and consultants who are skilled in identifying the basic problem areas of providing accessible web content for all. Our accessibility auditors and consultants ensure that the content is equally interactive and engaging for all the users. We conduct surveys to study the target audience and their consumption patterns to achieve a deeper understanding of the market. Our auditors are well-researched and have a deep understanding of the accessibility standards that helps them in auditing the content and making it more understandable for the users. We use a combination of automated tools and manual testing techniques to enhance our accessibility auditing services.
We provide a detailed assessment of content to identify the improvements vital to achieving compliance with WCAG 2.0 guidelines. We deliver conformance auditing and comply with the global auditing standards to evaluate whether a web content qualifies to be accessible to the users using Assistive Technology. We are experts in Australian Accessible ICT Standard AS EN 301 549:2016 as well as ADA and Section 508 standards of the United States.

Delivering accessibility audit services by evaluating how the web content and product supports the need of users with disabilities.

AODA Testing

SPZ is one of the leading digital accessibility companies around the globe. We have an experienced team of accessibility experts, legal counselors, and evaluation specialists that helps clients to achieve 100 percent AODA compliance. We cover all the five standards of AODA that include Customer Services, Information and Communication, Employment, Transportation, and Design of Public Spaces. We use the best practices for the web pages for WCAG and AODA guidelines to make the web content fully accessible. Hence, we make the web pages perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for disabled citizens.
Our services are not limited to making the websites accessible. We regularly monitor the performance of the websites. We guarantee Level AA conformance of accessibility and may even rise to Level AAA on the client's request. Higher levels of accessibility make the websites more SEO-friendly. Our experts offer accessibility services for HTML/XML pages, docs, pdf, forms, and all other types of documents. Our services are affordable, regular, and offer 24x7 client support.

VPAT Certification

SPZ is the well known name among leading business organizations for VPAT certification worldwide. Here, our team holds expertise over WCAG 2.0 and Section 508. Moreover, our platform is equipped with all relevant resources to make accessibility compliance products and services. At SPZ, our certified experts evaluate products’ functionality and determine accessibility standards that suit clients. Overall, we follow an efficient methodology to produce ACR. With us, clients receive 24/7 support and solutions for higher ROI.

A conformance report adds the brand value of companies. Moreover, it provides several other beneficial outcomes like:

  • Help in developing final products or services accessible
  • Allow clients to develop device responsive digital accessibility solutions
  • Make companies accountable for accessibility
  • The reports help companies in maintaining template editions (INT, VPAT 2.4 508, VPAT 2.4 WCAG, and VPAT 2.4 EU)
  • Multinational companies get ease in trade due to meeting all accessibility standards
